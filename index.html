Welcome to MCA

Our Therapeutic Philosophy

At Montana Counseling and Assessment, we believe in a holistic, individualized approach. Our therapeutic philosophy combines a variety of cutting-edge and evidence-based techniques with compassion, warmth, and a strengths-based perspective.

Family & Child Focus

We specialize in providing sensitive and effective psychological services to children, adolescents, and families struggling with a wide variety of life challenges.

Evidence-Based

Our methods are grounded in the latest psychological research, ensuring that our clients receive treatments that are proven to be effective and reliable.

Warm Environment

We strive to create a safe, non-judgmental space where clients feel comfortable exploring their thoughts and emotions at their own pace.

Counseling Services

Individualized therapy for children, adolescents, and families. We help navigate anxiety, depression, behavioral challenges, and significant life transitions using a tailored, strengths-based approach.

  • Family Counseling

Psychological Assessment

  • Diagnostic Evaluations
  • Treatment Planning
  • Cognitive & Behavioral Testing

Education & Training Services

Beyond individual clinical care, MCA staff provide specialized educational and training services to schools, agencies, and organizations seeking to improve their foundational knowledge and clinical approach.

Psychological Trauma

Trauma-informed care training and recognizing signs of distress.

Autism Spectrum Disorders

Best practices for support, integration, and understanding neurodivergence.

Child/Adolescent Health & Development

Guidance on developmental milestones and behavioral management strategies.
